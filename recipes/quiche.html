Quiche

Try this easy quiche recipe for breakfast, brunch, lunch, or dinner. Loaded with broccoli, ham, and Parmesan cheese, this quiche is totally crustless! A bit of baking mix in the batter firms up the egg mixture so it has no problems setting without a crust. Use other veggies or breakfast meats to make this your own.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of milk
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup of grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Lightly grease a 10-inch quiche dish.
  2. Beat milk, eggs, Parmesan cheese, baking mix, and butter in a large bowl until well combined; batter will be lumpy. Stir in broccoli, Cheddar cheese, and ham.
  3. Pour into the prepared quiche dish.
